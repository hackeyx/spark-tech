    Text2Speech  

      Text2speech is a Desktop application created by our CEO Salami Samad to help content creators or whoever to convert their speech to other languages and other voices. It has 10 different voices to choose from with 5 different languages. you have to download some of the voices before you can use them (the instructions to download other voices is in the updated application) but as default you can use just three voices provided you are a windows user (samad, joseph and mercy which are all english voices). it can also extract the words from a PDF (provided the pdf is 2000 KB or less) and say it out in your preffered voice. Note every audio produced by the app is saved within Text2speech folder. (The folder where the executable app is located.)
